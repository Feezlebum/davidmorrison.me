About

I got into crypto in 2014 and never really left. What started as curiosity turned into a career. I've spent the years since then managing forums, running social accounts, building Discord servers from zero members, and figuring out how to get people to actually care about technical products.

At NEAR Foundation I worked with co-founder Illia Polosukhin on brand strategy and communications. Illia co-authored "Attention Is All You Need", the research paper behind transformer models. We grew NEAR's social presence by 500% in six months. I also ran the largest CoinGecko co-marketing campaign in the ecosystem: 20,000 users, 10,000+ on-chain transactions.

The work is always the same at its core. Listen to what people actually need. Translate that back to the team building the product. Make the connection between the two groups feel real, not performative. I've done this in crypto and fintech. The energy sector has the same problem: complex product, sceptical audience, trust matters more than hype.
