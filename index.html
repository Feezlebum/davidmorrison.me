About

I've been building communities since before it was a job title. Since 2014, I've gone from managing forums to leading social media strategy for some of the most ambitious projects in blockchain — growing audiences from zero to hundreds of thousands, and turning passive followers into genuine advocates.

At NEAR Foundation, I worked directly with co-founder Illia Polosukhin — co-author of "Attention Is All You Need," the paper that invented the transformer architecture powering every major AI model today — on brand strategy and positioning. I've developed company-wide social strategies that delivered 500% growth in six months, launched co-marketing campaigns that drove 20,000 users on-chain, and mentored team members from day one to full autonomy.

What I do isn't complicated to explain, but it's hard to do well: I listen to communities, translate what they need into action, and make sure the people building things and the people using them are genuinely connected. I've done it in crypto, fintech, and Web3. Now I'm looking to bring that to energy — because the transition ahead needs people who can build trust, not just awareness.
